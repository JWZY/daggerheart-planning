# Nellie Saddler

**Ancestry:** Halfling

## Appearance
- Halfling with light skin, purple eyes and lots of freckles
- Short, stocky build
- Sandy blonde hair kept in a braid and/or tight bun
- Outfit is comfortable and practical, with pants, big pockets usually stuffed with miscellaneous items, a buttoned shirt, and HUGE circular glasses that take up approximately Â½ her face
- Often found lost in a book or mixing herbs with her mortar/pestle

## Backstory
- Medicine woman who came into town preaching about natural remedies and cures, rather than spiritual cleansing
- Tried to set up shop but was boycotted by a local spiritual group
- When she wouldn't back down, Nellie's shop was burnt to the ground in protest
- Joined up with the Caravan as it was passing through to the docks
- Hoping that she can build a new life for herself in Bonetop, in a town with modern ideals
- Will attempt to recruit people into the Modernistic Medical Society, which only has one member.... her.
- Young and feels like she has much to prove

---

**Occupation:** "Doctor", though she never completed her schooling for a legal "doctorate" title.

**Faction:** MMA (Modernistic Medical Association)

**Weaknesses:** Short-tempered, especially when insulted for choosing holistic remedies, overly insistent on proving herself, and any shelf above 4 feet.

**Inspirations:** Sadie (RDR2), Laura Ingalls (Little House), Scully (X-Files)
